☕ Ko-fi Art Shop Setup

Your Ko-fi page (ko-fi.com/meekotharaccoon) shows your $1 guides but not the art prints because the art hasn't been added to the Ko-fi Shop yet. This page has the exact copy-paste for all 7 art listings. Takes 15 minutes total.

Why art prints don't show up when people click your Ko-fi link

Ko-fi Shop products are separate from the Ko-fi "Support" button. Your guides ($1 Build Your Own Local AI Agent, etc.) are in your Shop. The 7 Palestinian art prints need to be added as separate Ko-fi Shop items. Once added, clicking "Buy a $1 art print" on Ko-fi will show ONLY the art.

How to Add Each Art Print to Ko-fi Shop (1 print = 3 minutes)

1
Go to ko-fi.com/meekotharaccoon → Click Manage Your Page
2
Click Shop in the left sidebar → Add Product
3
Type: Digital Item → paste the title, description, and price from each card below
4
Upload a preview image (use the emoji art from the gallery or any placeholder) → Set price to $1.00
5
For "File to deliver": upload a text file that says "Thank you! DM Meeko on Ko-fi for your print file." (until the real files are generated)
6
Publish → repeat for all 7 prints below
