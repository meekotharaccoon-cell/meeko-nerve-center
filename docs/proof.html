How Gaza Donations Work

  • Every product has a clearly stated Gaza split percentage (15% for guides, 70% for art prints)
  • Sales arrive via Ko-fi, Gumroad, or PayPal.me — all to Meeko's accounts
  • SolarPunk tracks accumulated Gaza funds in data/proof_ledger.json (public on GitHub)
  • When the Gaza fund reaches a threshold ($10+), Meeko manually transfers to PCRF and posts the confirmation number publicly — right here
  • We stop before showing: bank account numbers, PayPal login, full transaction IDs, or anything that could compromise account security
  • What we do show: PCRF confirmation email screenshot (with sensitive numbers redacted), total amount, date
⛔ STOP Protocol — How SolarPunk handles sensitive info When a donation transfer is made, the system logs: date, amount, and the PCRF confirmation reference. It does NOT log: PayPal transaction IDs, bank details, or login information. This file is public. The system is designed to be transparent about what went where, not about how to access the accounts.
