As the world grapples with the complexities of emerging technologies, it is imperative that we harness their potential to promote human rights, accountability, and transparency. The Open Society Foundations' Emerging Technology Initiative presents a unique opportunity to leverage technology in service of these values, and we are excited to submit our project, Meeko Nerve Center / SolarPunk, for consideration. With a focus on congressional accountability and Palestinian solidarity, our self-evolving AI platform embodies the principles of transparency, accountability, and civil society that are at the heart of your mission.

By developing an autonomous system that can build its own engines and operate with minimal infrastructure costs, we have created a scalable and sustainable model for promoting democracy and human rights. Our platform has already demonstrated its capabilities, with over 165 active engines and a fully open-source, AGPL-3.0 licensed framework that allows for seamless collaboration and forkability. As a testament to our commitment to transparency, our project is hosted on GitHub, where it can be accessed and contributed to by a global community of developers and advocates.

The Meeko Nerve Center / SolarPunk project is deeply rooted in the values of civil society, and we believe that it has the potential to make a meaningful impact in the lives of marginalized communities, particularly in Palestine. By generating art pieces and raising funds for organizations like the Palestine Children's Relief Fund, we aim to promote solidarity and support for the Palestinian people. Our project also seeks to promote accountability in the US Congress, ensuring that elected officials are held to the highest standards of transparency and integrity.

We are seeking funding of $100,000 to further develop and expand the capabilities of our platform, with a focus on integrating additional features and tools that can enhance its impact and reach. This funding will enable us to dedicate more resources to the project, including hiring additional developers and advocates to help drive its growth and adoption. With the support of the Open Society Foundations, we are confident that we can take our project to the next level, promoting human rights, accountability, and transparency in the US and around the world. We look forward to the opportunity to discuss our project in further detail and explore how we can work together to advance our shared values.
