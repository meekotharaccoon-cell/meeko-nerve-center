# AI Factory Framework
From: https://docs.axone.xyz/getting-started/core-concepts/ai-factory [citation:9]

## Components
- On-chain governance templates
- Resource management (datasets, models, compute)
- Identity management (SSI, SSO)
- Token-based contributor rewards
- Decentralized training coordination

## How to Use
1. Define zone rules (governance template)
2. Identify resources in Dataverse
3. Configure compute/storage providers
4. Train models collaboratively
5. Distribute rewards via tokens

## Connectors Available
- IPFS / Arweave for storage
- Akash for compute
- Cheqd for SSI
- Multiple blockchain integrations
